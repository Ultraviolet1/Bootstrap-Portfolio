About Me

Hello, I currently am a substitute teacher for Morgan Hunter Education Services and respit care provider for the Down Syndrom Guild Community in the Kansas City area. I have a Bachelor's degree in Speech Pathology and have been working in the academic environment with students ages 3-14 for 8+ years. My wonderful husband, Chad and I are huge gamers. We enjoy board games such as 7 Wonders, Gloomhaven and Terriforming Mars as well as online games by Blizzard Entertainment. I love games so much, I wanted to learn more about making them. So, with no background in technology or coding, I began the KU coding bootcamp to learn more. I look forward to all the new developer skills I will gain in the program and am ready to put in the hard work.
